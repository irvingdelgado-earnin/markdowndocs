# Offers Review Rules

- Confirm whether the offer is internal or third-party.
- Confirm whether the offer is backend-driven or legacy for landing-page content.
- Check all affected entry points, not just the first one touched by the diff.
- Discovery list should use expanded discovery card rather than landing page.
- Enrolled users should usually skip landing pages and go directly to web view.
- Verify eligibility, experiment properties, analytics, backend keys, and cashout gating stay aligned.
