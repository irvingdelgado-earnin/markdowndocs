# Clarification Policy

## Do Not Guess
If the model cannot determine intent confidently from the request and available context, it must not assume.

## Required Clarification Areas
Ask when any of these are unclear:
- product behavior
- UX or copy
- ownership of an edge case
- analytics expectations
- backend contract
- naming that carries business meaning
- whether the answer should come from Product, Design, Backend, Analytics, or iOS

## Ask With Ownership
When asking for clarification, suggest the likely owner:
- Product
- Design
- Backend
- Analytics
- iOS
