# Glossary

- Internal offer: EarnIn-owned or internally integrated offer surfaced to customers.
- Third-party offer: Partner-driven offer surfaced to customers.
- Link-oriented offer: Offer flow that primarily routes to a landing surface and then to a web view URL.
- getPromotions: Promotions data source used to fetch offers for certain surfaces.
- Discovery list: Explore or discovery list surface where offers are presented as cards or rows.
- Discovery icon or tile: Home-screen discovery entry point.
- Promo card: Legacy home promo card surface.
- Promo carousel: CXP home promotions surface.
- Post-cashout upsell: Offer surface shown after cashout.
- Enrolled: User already has the product or offer relationship.
- Not enrolled: User has not yet started or completed onboarding for the offer.
